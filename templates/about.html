About This Project

The Global Environmental Monitor is a comprehensive platform for visualizing and analyzing critical environmental data from around the world. Our mission is to make complex environmental information accessible and understandable through interactive visualizations and country-specific insights.

Data Sources

  • Electricity Usage Data: Provides information on electricity demand and per capita usage across countries and years.
  • Oil Consumption Data: Tracks global oil consumption trends measured in terawatt-hours.
  • Environmental Performance Index (EPI): Measures environmental health and ecosystem vitality across nations.
  • Earthquake Data: Records earthquake frequency by country and year.

Tools & Technologies

  • Backend: Python with Flask
  • Data Processing: Pandas
  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Data Visualization: Chart.js
